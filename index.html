AL-MUKARAM ESTATE

Brief History

Al-Mukaram Estate is located in the very heart of south c phase 4.The estate was established in the year 2006 and has since then undergone major changes from infrastructure to population. From lacking constant water supply to facing major electrical problems to having poor drainage and sewages running everywhere in the estate, the estate has since corrected every possible defect and is now an example to be emulated by other estates in south

Values of the estate

The estate is founded on values of islam and brotherhood and every resident of the estate is responsible for the outview of the estate.
